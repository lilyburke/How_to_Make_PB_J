How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Steps:

1. Gather materials (bread, peanut butter, jelly, & a knife)

2. Get two slices of bread

3. Apply peanut butter on one slice

4. Apply jelly on the other slice

5. Put the two slices together

6. Cut to your preference (square, triangle, circle etc.)

7. Eat and enjoy!!
